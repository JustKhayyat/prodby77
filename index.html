About

Minimal energy. Maximum frequency.

77 is a visionary producer and founder of SVNBIRDS, designing immersive soundscapes that bridge underground grit with cinematic polish. With a decade of studio mastery, 77 shapes artists’ stories into resonant experiences felt from club floors to curated playlists worldwide.

Each collaboration is a dialogue in rhythm—tailored arrangements, surgically engineered drums, and tonal palettes that evolve with every beat. Precision, emotion, and innovation define the catalog.
